Welcome to Bethany's Pie Shop

History

For many years, Bethany has been baking the most delicious pies at her home. In 2015, she opened her first store in Brussels. Delightful in a cozy little street near the Grand Market, people from all over the city fell in love with the hot cookies, heavenly cheese cakes, spicy apple pies... The store became a success in no time.

In 2015, no less than 100 pies were sold every day and Bethany's Pie Shop moved to a larger building. After receiving many requests, it became clear that our next step was making it possible to order pies from the comfort of your home from and from anywhere in the world. We are now making all our products in the very best stores. You will notice it in our fresh, user-friendly website, making it possible to order Bethany's delicious pies wherever you feel like it!

Bethany is famous for using only the best ingredients. This way, we can make the best tart. Our pies are absolutely the best!
